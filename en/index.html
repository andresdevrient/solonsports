Solon Sports Philosophy

Solon was considered one of the 'Seven Wise Men of Greece', in his philosophical and political conception he believed in freedom and individual rights, abolishing slavery. This figure leads us to humanize the athlete and not consider him just a commercial product. In this way Solon Sports was born and integrates psychological, sociological and sports analysis tools with the philosophy of preserving the mental, physical and spiritual health of each athlete.
